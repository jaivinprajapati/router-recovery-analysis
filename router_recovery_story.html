The Issue
  • Mar 1–16 cohort (21-day window expired) recovery rate: 58.2%
  • Down from 63.8% in Jan — a 5.6pp decline over 3 months
  • Small partners own ~36% of tickets and show steepest decline: 55.7% to 46.2%

  • Active partners own >95% of ticket volume — the problem sits entirely with them
What We Checked
  • Partner status split — Active vs Termination vs Suspended
  • Device bonus earning pattern — who earns consistently vs who stopped
  • Customer base size — Large vs Mid vs Small partners
  • City — Delhi vs Mumbai vs Bharat
  • Tenure — Early / Established / Seasoned / Mature / Legacy
  • Ticket-level recovery rate by each cohort — contribution to overall decline
  • Router Sikka feature — score-based bonus exception path
What We Found
  • Decline is broad-based — all cohorts, all cities, all tenure buckets declining
  • Small partners show biggest drop (−9.5pp) and overlap with Bharat — a composition effect
  • 89 "Declining" partners hold only 5% of tickets — not enough to explain the full decline
  • Router Sikka: only 11–133 partners affected — not a driver
  • Even Consistent bonus earners dropped 6.5pp. The issue is systemic.
Conclusion

Recovery decline is not concentrated in any segment — it cuts across size, city, tenure, and bonus performance cohort. Even top-performing partners are declining. This points to a systemic operational or process change between Jan and Feb 2026. Next step: RCA at the ticket level — what changed in Feb?

Conclusion — The Problem is Systemic

Finding
  • Small partners (36% of tickets) show the steepest decline: 55.7% to 46.2% (−9.5pp).
  • Bharat declines fastest (−10.5pp) but overlaps with Small — likely the same root cause.
  • All tenure buckets declining — no group is insulated.
  • Even Consistent bonus earners (top performers, 90%+ recovery) dropped 6.5pp — ruling out partner motivation or capability as the cause.
  • The decline is systemic — something changed at the operational or process level between Jan and Feb 2026.

Chapter 5

Router Sikka Feature — Ruled Out as Driver

Router Sikka is a feature that allows partners whose 1-month or 3-month device recovery rate is below 80% to still earn their device bonus — if they complete a defined set of recovery tickets surfaced to them in the Sikka interface. The backend marker for this is routerPickupBonusBlocked: score_eligible=True in T_TRANSACTIONS.EXTRA_DATA.

Ruled Out

The Sikka route is active for at most 133 partners in a month (Dec launch spike) and declining fast to 11 by Feb. With 1,195 active partners and a 9pp overall recovery decline, Sikka is not a meaningful driver of the trend. The Dec spike was a one-time backlog of borderline partners being flagged for the first time when the feature went live. Post-launch counts are negligible.
